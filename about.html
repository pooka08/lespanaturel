What We Do

A great event is always talked about and never forgotten. It becomes the ‘one to beat’ amongst attendees and everyone else that they share their experience with. Dulce&Co. specializes in creating those events!

 

Our event planning services guide you through every step of your big day, whether it is a wedding, birthday party, launch, charity event or mixer, including advice on venue, décor, timeline, food and drink, and whatever other details are going to be unique to your event.

 

We offer a range of services to help your event match your vision, although they are best used when combined, we are sure to include each services when planning your event.

 

Come and celebrate with style. Sweet life. Sweet memories.
